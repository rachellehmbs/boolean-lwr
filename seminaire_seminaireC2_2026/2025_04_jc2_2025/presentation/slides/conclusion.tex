\begin{mybox}[cblue]{Cyclotomic mappings and APNness}
\begin{itemize}

	\item[-] \blue{WANTED} : more necessary conditions to be APN (in the quadratic case).

\begin{itemize}
	\item[-] A lot of them are cyclotomic mappings $\leadsto$ is the zoo \red{that broad after all} ?
	\item[-] \red{PROBLEM} : geometrical structure \red{not} CCZ-invariant
	\item[-] Some ideas to detect it. But can we prove it ?
\end{itemize}
\end{mybox}

\begin{itemize}
	\item[-] Most of the APN functions found are \red{not} cyclotomic (at first sight)
	\item[-] Cyclotomic (with more conditions) seems a good search heuristic !
\end{itemize}
